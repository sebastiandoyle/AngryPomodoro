Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Angry Pomodoro is a simple, offline-focused productivity timer. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data. The app operates entirely on-device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account, email, or identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No location data

Device Permissions

Camera: Used only to access the device torch (flash) for visual alerts when you pick up the phone during a work session. The app does not record or save photos or video.

Audio: Used to play an alert sound when you break a session. No microphone access is requested.

Third-Party Services

None. Angry Pomodoro does not use third-party SDKs or send data to external servers.
